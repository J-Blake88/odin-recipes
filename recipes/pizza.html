Pizza

pepperoni is one of the most popular pizza toppings. Mixed with the melting cheese an sauce

Perfection

easy to follow an baic ingredients for a delicious at home pepperoni pizza in these 6 steps.

Ingredients

  • pizza sauce {home made or store bought}
  • pizza dough {home made or store bought}
  • mozzarella or other cheese
  • pepperoni
    •

    Steps

    1. pre heat oven to 350-400
    2. Roll the dough out to desired form
    3. Apply sauce
    4. Apply cheese
    5. Apply pepperoni
    6. place in oven for 25-30 minutes or until crust is lightly brown
